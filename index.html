About me

My name is Devin, but I'm also known as devin_real or intergrav. I enjoy UI/UX design, programming, music, and making useful things for free. I love open-source software and Linux. I currently maintain some Minecraft modpacks and a large library of carefully designed badges. This site was easily made with the help of new.css.

Projects

  • Devin's Badges - Popular, beautifully designed, and open badge library fit for many different services and projects. Also has a customizable template for your own use!
  • Adrenaline - Leading, lightweight client-side Minecraft modpack that aims to make your game faster in rendering and logic while keeping stability and simplicity.
  • Additive - Open-source and modular alternative to OptiFine in form of a Minecraft modpack for modern loaders such as Fabric and Quilt, built from Adrenaline.
  • DeckCraft - Minecraft modpack designed for the Steam Deck that attempts to provide a smooth OOBE with a major performance improvement and controller support.
